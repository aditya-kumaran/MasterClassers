Ocean’s Eight (2018)

Rewatchable

Metrics

Genre:

Comedy, Thriller

Rating:

7.7 /10

Audience:

Pretty much everyone, but it's a heist film, so people who like that. People who like the other Ocean's movies.

Rewatch Time:

Only 1.5h and pretty light, so could be once a week.

Winner:

Diane Kluger (Anne Hathaway)

Review

So I watched this after Ocean's 11,12, and 13, and I didn't expect it to live up to the others (which I did like), but it was actually really watchable. The casting in particular was great - Sandra Bullock and Cate Blanchett have great chemistry, Anna Hathaway is great, there's lots of diversity in Mindy Kalling and Awkwafina (both of whom acted the best I've seen from them). Helena Bonham Carter, Sarah Paulson, and Rihanna were all funny and the plot itself wasn't half bad. It didn't feel like a deliberately “girl-power” movie either, and it was great to see an ensemble cast of just women (and James Corden). Definitely worth a rewatch.
