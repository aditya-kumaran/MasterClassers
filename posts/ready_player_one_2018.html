Spoiler Territory

So Nolan Sorrento (Ben Mendelsohn) and IOI weren't awful villains - they were the antithesis to what the gamers stood for. Makes sense. His final hesitant moment at the end, and his persistent tentativeness to get his hands dirty, made him believable as just a corporate money-seeker. F'Nale - I swear I never heard her name in the movie - was cool too, even if it was kinda sad to see her get OHKOed at the end. The Samantha birthmark thing was cool, and Wade's aunt and uncle did die, so there are a few loose ends that were kinda glossed over. H (Lena Waithe) as a big dude was pretty funny too.
