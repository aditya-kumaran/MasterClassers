Review

So this one was a nice parody of the first one, with way way less charm and originality. I think they focused on way too many advocacy issues with the characters, and that really took away from the enjoyment of the story. That, and Eddie Murphy works a lot better as the main protagonist as opposed to the antagonist. The funeral scene was super iconic in the start, and I think all the cameos from black celebrities were really cool. Rehashing the barbershop, reverend, McDowell’s, etc. was all okay, I guess.
