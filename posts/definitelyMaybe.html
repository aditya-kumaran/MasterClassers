Review

So yeah, Ryan Reynolds and his daughter (Abigail Breslin) just have fun trying to out-quip each other in a game of ‘How I Met Your Mother’. It is funny, but there are so many outrageous jokes. There’s a solid cast, with Rachel Weisz and Elizabeth Banks, who are both great too. It suffers the same romcom pitfalls as most, and it does drag on a little bit (2h), and has a LOT of endings (which is funnily most peoples’ criticism of HIMYM). Still, Reynolds is funny, and the daughter is funny too.
