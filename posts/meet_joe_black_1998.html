Meet Joe Black (1998)

Rewatchable

Winner:

Joe Black (Brad Pitt)

Review

I did quite like it, even if it was longer than it needed to be. The office scenes were all pretty funny, and the supporting cast were all memorable enough not to be boring. The dynamic between Bill (Anthony Hopkins) and Joe was pretty cool to see, especially since the actors usually do different kinds of movies, and Susan (Claire Forlani) is cool too. The first scene with her meeting Joe is cute, and all of his fish-out-of-water stuff is pretty endearing, even if most of it is just Brad Pitt looking like Brad Pitt. Still, all the peanut butter stuff, “death and taxes” and everything was funny. Final scene with the fireworks was really nice, even if the ending might have been a little confusing for some people.
