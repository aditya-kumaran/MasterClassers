Review

So this is the movie with the boombox outside the window. Not gonna lie, I was originally only watching for that scene, but it's not even played up so much here. Still iconic though. This is a weird romance movie because it pretty much doesn't have any of the classic tropes that we're used to - it's a regular guy and girl just hanging out for most of the movie. Lloyd and Diane Court (Ione Sky, awesome name, really cute) have really cute chemistry, and the writers manage to make the dialogue very conversable - you wouldn't feel weird saying it to a friend. I think the subplot with Diane's dad (John Mahoney) was pretty auxiliary, and Lloyd's friends were really funny. So yeah, good movie to watch once - it's comforting and subverts your romance expectations in a wholesome way.
