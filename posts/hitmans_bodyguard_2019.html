Hitman's Bodyguard (2019)

Rewatchable

Winner:

Sonia (Salma Hayek)

Review

This movie is what you'd expect from a Sam Jackson and Ryan Reynolds movie. The action is entertaining, there's a bit of heart, and they have decent chemistry. Between Elodie Yung and Salma Hayek, the cast are pretty good looking, and the comedy is definitely worth a watch. Lots of swearing, as you'd think, and some decent dialogue from Jackson.

Spoiler Territory

Not really spoiler-sensitive.
