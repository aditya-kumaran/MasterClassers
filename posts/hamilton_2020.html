Hamilton (2020)

Council Class

Metrics

Genre:

Hollywood, Drama, (Historical), (Musical/Stage Play)

Rating:

8.8 /10

Audience:

People who like musicals, people who are interested in history. Americans, and people who have lots of patience. There's some implicit stuff that might not be great for kids.

Rewatch Time:

Not frequently, maybe once a year max. Listen to the songs, but it's 2h40.

Winner:

King George III (Jonathan Groff)

Review

Well I'm not a broadway guy, but I appreciate vocals, and Lin Manuel Miranda is a certified legend. From the opening scene (which kinda spoils itself) the music is great, and all the performers really fit their parts wonderfully. Miranda is great and captivating as Alexander Hamilton. Thomas Jefferson (Daveed Diggs) and George Washington (Christopher Jackson) are really fitting as the respected presidents, while Leslie Odom Jr.'s Aaron Burr is a pleasant surprise (but also the character that takes the most Ls). Jonathan Groff's King George III was a really nice comedic angle, reminiscent of Shakespeare's Puck. Most characters had really fulfilling and believable arcs, and I feel like I learned a bit, so that's good. If you're going to give oscars to any non-commercial movies, this is the way to go.

Spoiler Territory

Not really spoiler-sensitive.
