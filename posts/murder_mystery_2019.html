Review

Have some time on your hands? Want a couple laughs? 1.5 hours is a fair trade off for this movie. A cast of Adam Sandler, Jennifer Aniston, Luke Evans, Gemma Arterton (etc.) make it pretty entertaining throughout. And although the movie's structure mocks that of classic murder mysteries, it does suffer (ironically) from some of the same cliches. But the chemistry between Aniston and Sandler, and the abundance of non-forced funny moments more than makes up for any kind of unoriginality in the plot. Sandler's moustache does kind of piss me off though.
