Class is in Session

Want casual, spoiler-free and post-watch reviews? You're in the right place.

MasterClassers review movies based on how enjoyable and enriching an experience it was to watch them, rather than critiquing their style. No matter how good a movie is, if you'd only watch it once, it's likely not worth as much as a movie you can watch multiple times.

Sorted into custom genres, each movie will be given a score out of 10 and placed into one of the categories below. You can read our impressions of the movie too, and if you've seen it, do check out the spoiler section.
